# Enclosure brief example

Prompt brief:

Create a two-piece enclosure for a small PCB and cable exit.

Requirements:
- internal cavity: 72 mm x 45 mm x 18 mm
- wall thickness: 2 mm
- removable lid with four screw posts
- USB-C cable exit on one short side
- clearance for a status LED window
- printable on a Bambu Lab printer with PLA or PETG

Ask for:
- exact PCB hole spacing
- USB-C opening dimensions
- desired screw size
- whether heat-set inserts are preferred

Deliver:
- phased previews during iteration
- final STL or 3MF
- orientation and support guidance
