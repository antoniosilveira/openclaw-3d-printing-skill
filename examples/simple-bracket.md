# Simple bracket example

Prompt brief:

Design a printable right-angle wall bracket for a lightweight sensor.

Requirements:
- vertical plate: 60 mm x 40 mm x 4 mm
- horizontal shelf: 50 mm deep x 30 mm wide x 4 mm
- two wall mounting holes for M4 screws, 40 mm apart vertically
- generous fillets at the inside corner
- optimized for PLA on a 0.4 mm nozzle
- no supports if possible

Deliver:
- STL
- preview image
- short print settings recommendation
